## Overview

As a product manager served at several technology companies, Feynman's responsibilities include:

- Design and deliver new features and capabilities to improve the user experience of commercial product portfolio
offerings based on the open-source projects.
- Collaborate with marketing/business teams to grow customer adoption and build partnership with external vendors

- Work with partner teams to determine the features and user experiences to prioritize in the product roadmap, eventually
meet the team's objectives and key results (OKRs).
